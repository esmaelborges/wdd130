Sal Island, part of Cape Verde, is renowned for its stunning white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and year-round sunshine. This flat island, once a salt mining hub, now thrives on tourism, offering diverse water sports and a welcoming atmosphere in its charming town of Santa Maria.
